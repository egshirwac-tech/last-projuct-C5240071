Help & Instructions

How to Play

1. Click "Start Quiz" on the home page to begin the quiz.

2. Each question has a 15-second timer. Answer before time runs out!

3. Select your answer by clicking on one of the options.

4. You'll see immediately if your answer was correct or incorrect.

5. After answering, click "Next Question" to proceed.

6. After completing all questions, your final score will be shown.

7. Submit your score to the leaderboard with your name and email.
